## 1. Concept

**Logline (1 sentence):**
*When caring for someone you love means juggling meds, appointments, and a hundred half-read texts, CareCircle gathers the whole family onto one calm, shared timeline — so no one carries it alone, and no detail slips away.*

**Brand voice:** Warm, calm, caring, reassuring, human. Sincere over slick. We speak the way you'd speak to a tired sibling at 11pm — gentle, certain, kind. Never clinical, never enterprise-y, never "hype."

**Palette:**
| Role | Value | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Accent (soft rose/red) | `#E8959B` / `#D97A82` | CTA, the "live" dot, source-cite highlight, logo |
| Warm cream / paper | `#FBF7F4` | Primary background, whitespace |
| Warm clay text | `#3D3A38` | Headlines, body |
| Soft sage support | `#A9B8A4` | Secondary accents, "synced" confirmations |
| Golden-hour light | `#F3D9C4` | Lighting wash, lens warmth |

Generous whitespace, soft drop shadows, large rounded corners (16–24px radii), longer eases (cubic-bezier 0.22, 1, 0.36, 1). Light grain over live-action for warmth.

## 2. Dual-Format Note (16:9 vs 9:16)

| Aspect | 16:9 (landing, YouTube, site hero) | 9:16 (Reels / TikTok / Stories) |
|---|---|---|
| **Crop / framing** | Wide; show full timeline + window chrome side-by-side. Hands-and-table in left third, rule of thirds. | Tight, vertical. Stack hero subject top, UI lower-center. Crop window chrome to a single column of the timeline. |
| **Text placement** | Lower-third subtitles; product UI centered with breathing room. | Subtitles in upper-middle safe zone (above thumb UI / caption bar). Big key phrases burned center. |
| **Pacing** | Slightly slower; let cross-dissolves breathe (avg shot 3–4s). | ~20% faster cuts to hold attention (avg shot 2–2.5s); hook compressed to land by 0:03. |
| **UI scale** | Real-size browser mock, cursor visible. | Zoom 130–150% into the active row; cursor replaced by a soft tap-glow. |
| **Captions** | Optional (audio-on assumed) | Always-on, sound-off-first design. |

## 6. Visual Direction

- **Transitions:** Cross-dissolves over hard cuts, always. The signature move: sticky notes / scattered texts literally *melt and reflow* into clean timeline cards (morph dissolve) at the 0:05 aha. Use light-wash dissolves (a soft golden bloom bridges shots).
- **Timeline animation:** Cards stagger-in bottom-up, 80ms apart, with a gentle scale (0.96→1) and fade. The vertical thread "draws" downward like ink settling. The rose "live" dot has a slow 2s pulse. "Synced" sage tick ripples outward once.
- **Q&A beat:** Query types in at a human cadence (~9 chars/sec, slight irregularity, no robotic monospace). After submit, a soft 3-dot shimmer (~0.8s), then the answer fades up line-by-line. The **source chip** is the hero: it lights last, in rose, with a brief 1.1x scale-pop and a faint underline-draw — the emotional proof that the answer is *trustworthy and from their own notes*.
- **Lighting:** Golden-hour, soft and directional, warm key from frame-left, gentle fill. Real-action shots graded warm (lifted shadows, creamy highlights). Light film grain (subtle, ~5%). Slight lens softness in corners.
- **Cursor / window chrome:** MacOS-style chrome with rounded corners and a soft drop shadow floating on the cream background. Cursor moves on eased curves (never linear), pauses before clicks, micro-overshoots like a real hand.
- **Type treatment:** Big phrases set in the humanist sans, generous tracking on the wordmark, lowercase warmth. Headlines breathe — never crowd the frame.

## 7. Audio Direction

- **Music:** Warm solo piano opening (felt/upright timbre), strings entering at 0:05 (the aha), building hopeful but never triumphant. Resolves to a single sustained warm chord at the CTA. **BPM ~72** (a resting-heart-rate calm), with a gentle lift to ~84 across the walkthrough, settling back to ~72 for the CTA. Major key, soft suspensions that resolve — emotionally "exhale."
- **SFX (all soft, low-level, never jarring):**
  - 0:01 muffled phone buzz (the pain)
  - 0:03 faint page-turn (sticky notes / paper)
  - 0:05 single soft chime (the aha)
  - 0:13 soft notification "tick" on the synced card
  - 0:21 light keystroke wash under the typing (subtle, not clacky)
  - 0:28 gentle "answer" chime as the source chip lights
- **VO tone:** Warm, human, mid-range, unhurried. Breathy intimacy, like spoken across a kitchen table. Mix VO slightly forward, music ducked −6dB under voice. No reverb showiness — keep it close and honest.
- **Mix philosophy:** Quiet is a feature. Leave audible breaths and small silences; they read as calm.

VO trims to lines at 0:00, 0:05, 0:14, 0:24. Cut the reminders/role line.

### 90-second cut (site hero / explainer)
Add breathing room + a light testimonial-style beat.
- **0:00–0:08** — Extended emotional hook: two siblings texting in different cities, a missed dose, the quiet worry. Slower.
- **0:08–0:20** — Aha + full Feature 1 with each card lingered on, role-based access shown clearly (family vs physician), reminders demonstrated end-to-end.
- **0:20–0:40** — Feature 2 expanded: TWO example questions answered from notes ("When's the next appointment?" / "What did the doctor say about the new dosage?"), each with its source chip — reinforce the RAG-over-your-own-notes trust story.
- **0:40–0:60** — Real-feeling moment: the family group, an update posted, everyone sees it at once across phones in different rooms/cities. "No one carries it alone."
- **0:60–0:80** — Gentle benefit recap over warm imagery: peace of mind, nothing slipping, everyone in sync.
- **0:80–0:90** — CTA, slower resolve, URL, "Start your circle today."

Add one optional on-screen line of social proof if available (e.g. "Built on 10,000+ care notes families trust"). Music gets a fuller arrangement with a mid-point breath before the final swell.
